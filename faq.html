Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions or get help below.

How do I make a booking?

Go to the Booking page, select your preferred bus, date, and time, then proceed to payment.

What payment methods are supported?

We support M-Pesa, Airtel Money, T-Kash, PayPal, and credit/debit cards.

How can I cancel my booking?

Navigate to the Past Bookings page, locate your booking, and select the cancel option.
